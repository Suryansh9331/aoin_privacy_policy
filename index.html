Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Aoin (“we”, “our”, “us”) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Aoin mobile application (“App”).

1. Information We Collect

User Accounts

  • First name
  • Last name
  • Mobile number
  • “Merchants may upload photos or videos (reels) as part of product or promotional content.”

This information is collected when users register or log in using OTP-based authentication.

Merchant Accounts

Merchants cannot register directly through the App. Merchant registration is completed on the Aoin website. Registered merchants can log in to the App using the same credentials.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage user and merchant accounts
  • To authenticate users using OTP verification
  • To provide access to app features
  • To ensure security and prevent unauthorized access
  • To improve app functionality

3. Purchases and Payments

Users may be redirected to the Aoin website to complete product purchases. The App does not process or store payment card details. All payments are handled securely on the website.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent personal data to third parties. Information may be shared only when required by law or to protect the security and integrity of the platform.

5. Data Security

We use reasonable security measures to protect personal information. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

6. Data Retention

Personal information is retained only as long as necessary to provide services or meet legal requirements.

7. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. User Rights

Users may request access, correction, or deletion of their personal information by contacting us.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
